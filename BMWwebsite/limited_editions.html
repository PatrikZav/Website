Frequently asked questions about the Limited editions of BMW M4.

How many cars were made for each limited edition BMW M4?

BMW M4 Kith - 150 cars

BMW M4 CSL - 1 000 cars

BMW M4 50 Jahre Edition - 500 cars

BMW M4 CS - 3 000 cars

BMW M4 GT3/GT4 - built on demand for racing teams

Are these models street legal?

Yes, most of them (Kith, CSL, CS, 50 Jahre) are fully street legal. However, GT3 and GT4 models are track-only race cars and are not road legal.

What makes these editions different form the regular BMW M4?

Limited edutuibs feature exclusive styling, race trims, carbon fiber elements, unique paint finishes, performance upgrades and lightweight components. For example, the M4 CSL is over 100 kg lighter than the M4 Competition

Why are these models so expensive?

Their price reflects exclusivity, design uniqueness, performace enhancements and collector value. Many of them appreciate over time, making them desirable not just for driving, but also for investment.

Can I still buy one today?

Not from BMW directly. All models were sold out quickly. Today, they can only be bought secondhand, often at a premium price due to their rarity.

Which model is the rarest?

BMW M4 Kith Edition - with only 150 cars worldwide, it's one of the most exclusive and visually distinctive M4s ever made.
